How It Works

ParkingFinder helps you locate free and paid parking options near you or any address worldwide. The app displays parking spots on a live map with color-coded pins — green for free, orange for paid — along with details like pricing, hours, capacity, and more.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why don't I see any parking spots on the map?

Try zooming in closer. To prevent the app from freezing, parking pins are hidden when you're zoomed out too far. You'll see a "Zoom in to see parking spots" message when this happens. You can also tap the "Search This Area" button after panning to a new location.

Why does the app need my location?

Your location is used to find parking options near you and show your position on the map. Location data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers.

How do I search for parking near a specific address?

Tap the search bar at the top of the map and start typing an address. Autocomplete suggestions will appear as you type. Tap a suggestion to navigate there and see nearby parking options.

How do I remove ads?

Go to the Settings tab and tap "Subscribe" to upgrade to Pro. This removes all banner ads from the app.

How do I restore my subscription on a new device?

Go to the Settings tab and tap "Restore Purchases." Your Pro subscription is tied to your Apple ID and will be restored automatically.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open your iPhone's Settings app, tap your name at the top, then tap Subscriptions. Find ParkingFinder Pro and tap Cancel Subscription. Your Pro benefits will continue until the end of your current billing period.

Is the parking data accurate?

Parking data is sourced from OpenStreetMap, a community-maintained global mapping database. While coverage is generally excellent in urban areas, data accuracy depends on local contributors. Some details like pricing or hours may not always be up to date.

Does the app work outside the United States?

Yes! ParkingFinder works worldwide. OpenStreetMap has global coverage, though data density varies by region. European cities tend to have particularly good parking data.

How do I save a favorite parking spot?

Tap any parking pin on the map, then tap the heart icon in the detail view. You can give it a custom name like "Work Garage" for easy reference later. Find all your saved spots in the Favorites tab.

I'm getting a "Too many requests" or timeout error.

The parking data server occasionally experiences high traffic. The app will automatically retry a few times. If the issue persists, wait a minute and try again.
