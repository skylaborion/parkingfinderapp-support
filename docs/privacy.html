Effective Date: March 12, 2026

ParkingFinder ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

Location Data

When you grant permission, the app accesses your device's location to display your position on the map and find nearby parking options. Your location data is processed on your device and is used solely to query the OpenStreetMap Overpass API for parking data near your coordinates. We do not store, track, or transmit your location to our own servers.

Search Queries

When you search for an address, the query is sent to Apple's MapKit services to provide search results and autocomplete suggestions. These requests are handled by Apple and are subject to Apple's privacy policy.

Favorites

Saved favorite parking spots are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is not transmitted to any external server.

Subscription Information

If you subscribe to ParkingFinder Pro, the transaction is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information, Apple ID, or billing details.

2. Third-Party Services

OpenStreetMap Overpass API

The app queries the OpenStreetMap Overpass API to retrieve parking data. These requests include a geographic bounding box (latitude and longitude coordinates of the area you're viewing) but do not include any personal information. OpenStreetMap's privacy policy applies to their processing of these requests.

Google AdMob

The free version of the app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect certain device information and use advertising identifiers to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your iPhone's Settings under Privacy & Security > Tracking. For more information, see Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Apple MapKit

Address search and map display are powered by Apple MapKit. Apple's privacy policy governs the handling of data by these services.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any personal identifiers
  • We do not create user accounts
  • We do not track your movements or build location profiles
  • We do not sell, share, or rent any data to third parties
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs beyond AdMob

4. Data Storage

All app data (favorites, preferences, filter settings) is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, this data is permanently removed.

5. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, there is no personal data for us to provide, modify, or delete. Your locally stored app data can be removed by deleting the app from your device.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
